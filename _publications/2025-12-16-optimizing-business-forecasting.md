This paper presents a theoretical framework and simulation study on optimizing business forecasting through human-AI decision fusion. The research explores how to effectively combine human judgment with AI predictions to improve forecasting accuracy and decision-making quality.

**Presented at:** International Conference on Information Systems (ICIS) 2025, Nashville, TN, USA
